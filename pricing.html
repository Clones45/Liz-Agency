FAQ

Common questions about pricing

Is ad spend included in the monthly price?

No — the monthly retainer covers our management, optimization, and system upkeep. Your ad spend budget is paid directly to the ad platform (Meta, Google, etc.) so you have full visibility into where it goes.

Is there a minimum contract?

Standard packages are month-to-month after setup. If you'd rather skip the setup fee, we also offer a $0-setup option in exchange for a longer minimum agreement.

What exactly does the setup fee cover?

The one-time build: your dedicated CRM configuration, automation workflows, and initial ad account/campaign setup — the work required before your system can go live.

Can I upgrade packages later?

Yes — most clients start with Starter or Growth and add platforms, review automation, or an AI chatbot once the core system is proven out.

Do you work outside of these industries?

Our system is built specifically for local service businesses — contractors, dental/medical, real estate, legal, and home services are the best fit. Reach out and we'll tell you honestly if it's a good match.
