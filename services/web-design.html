FAQ

Common questions about web design

How long does a new website take?

Most marketing sites launch in 4–8 weeks depending on page count and content readiness. Ecommerce and custom builds typically run 8–12 weeks.

Do you handle hosting and domains?

Yes — we can manage hosting, domain, and SSL for you, or work within your existing setup if you'd rather keep control of it.

Can you redesign our site without losing our SEO rankings?

Yes. Every redesign includes a redirect map and technical SEO checklist so rankings and link equity carry over to the new site.

What platforms do you build on?

Most commonly WordPress and Shopify, plus fully custom builds when a project needs something off the shelf can't do.

Will I own the website?

Always. You own the code, content, and domain — there's no vendor lock-in.
