FAQ

Common questions about email & SMS

Do you use Go High Level (GHL)?

Yes — every client gets their own dedicated Go High Level sub-account as their CRM, pipeline, and communication hub. Leads land in it automatically and every automation runs on top of it.

Which platforms do you support?

Go High Level is the CRM and automation hub for every client. If you already run on Klaviyo or Mailchimp for ecommerce email, we can manage those too.

Do you write the copy and design the templates?

Yes — strategy, copy, and design are all included, built to match your brand rather than a generic template.

How do you handle compliance (CAN-SPAM, TCPA)?

Every campaign and flow follows CAN-SPAM and TCPA requirements — proper opt-ins, clear unsubscribe paths, and consent tracking for SMS.

How fast can flows go live?

Core flows (welcome, abandoned cart, post-purchase) typically launch within 2–3 weeks of onboarding.

Do you help grow the list too?

Yes — popups, on-site capture, and lead magnets can be layered in alongside your existing traffic and paid channels.
