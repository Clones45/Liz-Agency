FAQ

Common questions about paid advertising

What's the minimum ad budget to work with you?

We typically recommend a minimum of $2,000–$3,000/month in ad spend so campaigns have enough data to optimize against — management fees are separate from ad spend.

Which platforms do you run ads on?

Primarily Google Ads and Meta (Facebook/Instagram), plus shopping/catalog ads for ecommerce clients. We'll recommend the right mix based on where your customers actually are.

How do you measure success?

Against a CAC or ROAS target we set together up front — not vanity metrics like impressions or clicks. You get a live dashboard, not a monthly PDF.

Do you build the landing pages too?

Yes — dedicated, high-converting landing pages are included for major campaigns rather than sending traffic to a generic homepage.

Is there a long-term contract?

We ask for a 90-day minimum to let campaigns reach statistical significance, then it's month-to-month after that.
