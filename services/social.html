FAQ

Common questions about social media

Do you create the content, or just schedule it?

We create it — strategy, copywriting, design, and (for video) production — then schedule and manage it too.

Which platforms do you manage?

Most commonly Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, X, and TikTok. We'll recommend which platforms are actually worth your budget rather than spreading you thin across all of them.

Do you handle paid social too?

Yes — organic and paid social are planned together so ad spend amplifies content that's already proven to resonate.

How do you measure ROI on social?

Beyond followers and engagement, we track link clicks, leads, and (where trackable) attributed revenue back to specific posts and campaigns.

Can I approve content before it goes live?

Yes — every client gets a review step in the content calendar before anything is published.
